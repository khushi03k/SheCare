🍎 Health Tips

Stay healthy, strong, and confident every day 🌟

  • 🥗 Eat a balanced diet rich in iron, calcium, and fiber
  • 💧 Stay hydrated — aim for 8 glasses of water a day
  • 🧘 Practice regular exercise or yoga to reduce cramps and stress
  • 😴 Maintain a consistent sleep schedule (7–9 hours)
  • 🩸 Track your menstrual cycle to understand your body better
  • 👩‍⚕️ Get regular health checkups and screenings
  • 🧠 Take breaks and care for your mental health
  • 📵 Limit screen time before bed for better sleep quality
  • 🌞 Get sunlight in the morning to boost Vitamin D levels
  • 🧴 Use gentle, skin-safe products for hygiene and skincare
